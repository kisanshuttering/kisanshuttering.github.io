Step-by-Step Connection

  1. Create a new Google Form in Google Drive.
  2. Add questions for Name, Company, Mobile Number, Location (City), Material, Project Size, & Project Details.
  3. Ensure the Form's settings allow anyone to submit responses without sign-in.
  4. Paste your Google Form link above and click Auto-Map Fields.
  5. Our server will automatically configure mapping rules and instantly pipe new leads right into your Form database!
