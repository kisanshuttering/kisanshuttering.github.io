CIVIL ENGINEERING GUIDE

Material Planning & Safety Guidelines

Essential considerations for planning material loading, vertical support distribution, and scaffolding alignments at your construction site.

1

Slab Load & Support Calculation

Always support wet concrete with adequate prop jacks. Spacing should be based on slab thickness (typically 1 prop per 10-12 square feet for a standard 5-inch house slab).

2

Cuplock Scaffolding Alignment

Plumb standards perfectly on solid ground. Fit horizontal ledgers into node cups at every 0.5m-1.5m vertical span depending on structural loads.

3

Pre-Pour Civil Inspection Checklist

Verify cuplock node tightness, pin-locks, H-frame alignment, and MS challi walkboard connections before starting casting pour series.
