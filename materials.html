Technical Material Procurement Guidelines

Selecting Centering Plate Quantities

To accurately estimate steel centering plates on rent, calculate your total slab area in square feet and divide by the plate size area (6 sq. ft for 3x2 plates, and 8 sq. ft for 4x2 plates). Add a standard 5% layout border overlap margin to account for variable structural column edges and beam bottom adjustments.

Cuplock Spacing & Structural Load Rules

Under standard Indian civil structures, vertical cuplock standards should be spaced no further than 1.2 meters to 1.5 meters apart during slab casting heights of up to 4 meters. For double-height slabs or bridge girders, custom calculations using modular ledger alignments at 500mm nodes are mandatory to avoid structural vertical bucking risks.

Quality Compliance Inspections

Our yards operate a thorough checklist to ensure structural integrity: pipes are monitored for concentric gauge straightness under digital calipers, couplers are tracked for clean grease-lines and bolt threads, and base plates undergo stress verification welds to prevent sudden site shifts under concrete pressure.
